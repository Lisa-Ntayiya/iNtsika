Told Stories
iNtsika YeMfundo(background), 2020

Intsika YeMfundo(an Isixhosa name meaning "A Catalyst for Education") is an NPO essenntially all about assisting bright kids who come from impoverished households with University applications and entrance. The NPO founded by a group of friends who strongly believe that Africa's strength lies in her people, which is why it it not surprising that when they are not at work they spend most of the time assisting these bright students from rural areas with University entrances, career guidance and mentoring through this Organisation. The Organisation has been in existance for 6 years now and has already assisted more that 10 kids and placed them in various Universities in the Western Cape.
"We have worked with two high schools from our home town and two universities in Capetown.We thought that education is the most effective and impactful tool for us to use in giving back to our communities"
said the Founders.
